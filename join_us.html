How to Join

For Prospective Students

Thank you for your interest in our lab. If I have not replied to your message, I apologize. Unfortunately, I am not always able to respond to every inquiry individually. To learn more about our research, please explore the information provided on this website.

Please note that I cannot guarantee admission to the lab. The number of available positions depends on various factors such as funding and space, and all graduate admissions are managed centrally by the department rather than by individual faculty members.

For the Ph.D. program, I normally select students after observing their work during the two-year master’s program. Exceptions are made only in rare cases, for example when a student is exceptionally qualified or when there is a prearranged research collaboration or external funding.

For the master’s program, I currently accept students only through POSTECH’s Industrial Data Science (IDS) program, except in rare cases involving exceptional qualifications or special arrangements.

Undergraduate research opportunities are available only to Postechians. If you are a Postechian, you are welcome to visit my office, with or without an appointment.

Recommend Background

If you would like to join our lab, you should have a strong background in statistics, data science, machine learning, or a related field. This includes undergraduate-level knowledge of linear algebra, probability theory, deep learning, computer programming, and similar topics.

In addition, if you are interested in the Ph.D. program, a strong background in mathematical analysis and mathematical statistics is highly recommended.
