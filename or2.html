Operational Research II (IMEN 266)

Course Objectives

This course covers some basic modeling tools to handle probabilistic system such as Markov chains, Poisson processes, and continuous-time Markov chains. Conditional probability and conditional expectation are reviewed with some examples. Renewal, queueing and reliability theories will also be considered.

Textbook

  • Sheldon M. Ross. (2014) Introduction to Probability Models. (11th Edition). Academic Press
